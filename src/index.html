Welcome to the Dungeon of Sudo!

Before we begin, please pick your class!

Warrior!

The warrior has high HP and Strength!

Warrior abilities are:

  • Power Smash- do a lot of damage!
  • Double Strike- Attack twice!

Wizard!

The Wizard has high stamina, for casting spells!

Wizard spells are:

  • Fireball- deal damage to all enemies!
  • Lightning Bolt - deal high damage to one enemy!

Thief!

The thief has high dexterity, for being tricky!

Thief abilities are:

  • Riposte- deal damage while dodging
  • Sneaky Stab- deal extra damage
